## Account Balance

Your remaining balance is displayed at the top of the billing page along with options to top up credits or add a payment method. A payment method must be added via **Setup** before top-ups and recurring billing can be processed.

## Pay As You Go Pricing

The default plan offers flexible search with flexible pricing and no monthly commitment.

| Endpoint | Price | Calculation |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Search | $8.00 / 1k requests* | 0.1 credits fixed + 0.01 credit for every result |

*Calculated for up to 10 results per request.

## Plans & Pricing

Starter and Pro plans offer multiple credit tiers at the same per-credit rate. Pick the tier that matches your expected monthly volume. Credits and monthly price scale together, while rate limits, support, and per-credit cost stay the same within a plan.

## Plan Details

### Pay As You Go

Get started with minimum commitment.

- Top up balance as needed.
- $0.04 per credit.
- Top-up credits.
- 50 requests per minute.
- Email support.

### Starter

Ideal for light developer use cases.

- From 500 credits / month, scalable up to 2,500.
- $0.036 / credit (10% off).
- Top-up credits.
- 100 requests per minute.
- Email support.

### Pro

Ideal for heavy production use cases.

- From 4,000 credits / month, scalable up to 15,000.
- $0.032 / credit (20% off).
- Top-up credits.
- 250 requests per minute.
- Priority support.

### Enterprise

Build custom endpoints entirely to your specification.

- Custom credits / month.
- Custom price per credit.
- De-duplicated feeds.
- Custom rate limits.
- Dedicated Account Manager.

## Guidelines and Notes

- Top up your balance at any time using the **Top up balance** action.
- A payment method must be added before top-ups can be processed.
- Higher-tier plans offer discounted per-credit pricing and increased rate limits.
- Within Starter and Pro, you can scale credits up or down at the next billing cycle.
- Plan upgrades take effect immediately; remaining balance carries over.
- For pricing discussions or custom plan adjustments, contact [sales@wokelo.ai](mailto:sales@wokelo.ai).
